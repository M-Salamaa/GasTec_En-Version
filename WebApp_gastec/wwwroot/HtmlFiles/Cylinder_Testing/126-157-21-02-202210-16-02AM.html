Cylinder Testing Steps

CNG Cylinder is considered one of the most important component of the conversion kit. GASTEC provides Cylinders Testing Service for all customers of VNG in Egypt throughout Cylinders Testing Centers affiliated to the Company in different governorates.

Cylinders Testing Department carries out tests at the Company’s centers according to Egyptian standard of requirements and specifications included number of international followed specifications, as well as calibrating the used devices in specifications periodically through accredited entities.

Here are the followed steps:

1-    Ensuring that the vehicle has been converted thorough one of the accredited authorities by previewing the certificate of origin, release and the charging card or work order addressed to a testing center from the accredited entity.

2-    Preparing the cylinder for testing.

3-    Discharging the cylinder from gas.

4-    Inspecting the outside surface after cleaning to ensure that there is no tap, corrosion, rust, or flaws, roasted, enlargement or any other defects in the cylinder’s body. In case of existing one of the aforementioned defects, the cylinder’s validity is determined according to manufacturing and inspection specifications of the cylinder or through the following specifications:

•    New Zealand Specification - NZS 5454.

•    Australian Specification – AS 2030 1 -1999.

•    The Egyptian Standard Specification.

•    Specification – ISO 11439 - Specification ISO 19078.

5-    Cylinder Weight: in case the Cylinder’s weight is less than 95% of the printed weight, the cylinder rejected.

6-    Inspection of screw: the internal screw of the cylinder is inspected to ensure its safety and free of any malformations in addition, the cylinder’s screw valve.

7-    Testing manufactured Cylinders according to Specification: ISO/FDIS 11439 through making hydrostatic test. As for testing manufactured cylinders as per New Zealand Specification and Australian Specification AS 2030 1 -1999 are being done through hydraulic pressure test and concerning the cylinders manufactured according to ASMI Specification the test is being done by Ultrasonic device and comparing it to thickness existed in its catalogue.

8- Cylinder Drying: through lifting cylinder from pressure device and setting on drying device, installing cables of drying device and then pressing on operation switch in order to start drying cycle, taking 6 minutes.

9- Painting: in case of rusting, the old painting of the rusted part is being removed by using Paint Remover and the whole cylinder is being repainted by white color.

10- Printing Testing Date: the testing date is being printed on the cylinder by the related pens.

11-  Assembling Cylinder in the Vehicle: the cylinder valve is being installed by torque switch, the cylinder is charged with CNG after being connected to vehicle, and the valve is being inspected by using water and soup so as to notice if there is any leak, after then the test certificate issued.

In case of cylinder’s invalidity, the cylinder is being scrapped by making a hole in the cylinder’s body about 5 cm also cut the cylinder’s neck as to be unusable anymore according to the followed specifications.  Accredited certificate is being issued; keeping a copy and handing over another copy to the customer.
