GASTEC provides cylinder testing service for all NGV customers in Egypt, through the company’s testing centers in (Cairo- Alexandria- Mansoura- Ismailia- Port said- Suez - Qena - Behira - Assuit) according to Egyptian requirements and specifications that include a number of international followed standards, as well as inspecting devices used in measurements periodically through accredited institutions.

During the first half of 2021, GASTEC tested (6485) cylinders, reaching a number of (242527) tested cylinders since the inception of cylinder testing activity in the Company in 2004.
