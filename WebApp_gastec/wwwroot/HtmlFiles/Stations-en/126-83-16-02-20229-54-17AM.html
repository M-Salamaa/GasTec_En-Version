Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition System "SCADA System"

GASTEC Co-operated with Information System and Computer Center affiliated to Military Production Ministry in order to implement and develop Automatic Linking Program Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition "SCADA System" for fueling stations  affiliated to the Company  for a number of  compressors with their inclusions including rapid development stations as a priority as well as the existing stations already since the Company’s establishment.

SCADA System provides monitoring performance rates at stations, daily sales, improving maintenance works for equipment, in addition to recording number of vehicles, gas amount consumed per each vehicle and final fueling pressure.

 

-The Modern Design of Fueling Stations

 

GASTEC developed CNG fueling techniques, installing for the first time the CNG facilities such as compressors and CNG tanks and other accessories in separate , availing yet vast areas to be used in various activities and services to the customers. The adoption of such an advanced design would help in developing the infrastructure of CNG fueling facilities and supporting the spread of CNG stations. In addition, the Company applied such upgraded techniques at Port Said and Beni Swif stations.

In addition to developing the current shape of the CNG dispensers and increasing safety rates through placing all their components above the fueling canopy so as to save area in the fueling zone.

Also, an integrated plan was made to spread such stations on a large-scale during the next five years in several cities, governorates, new Urban Communities and the national road grid.

The total number of CNG fueling stations affiliated to the Company reached 241 stations, representing the largest spread rate for fueling stations among all NGV companies in Egypt.
