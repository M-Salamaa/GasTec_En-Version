Integrated Model Fueling Services (Liquid Fuel, CNG and Electrical Charging)

In pursuance of GASTEC’s policy as to incorporating new activities contributing to maximizing the revenues, the Company signed an agreement with eni S.p.A in order to establish new fueling stations with liquid fuel carrying eni trademark countrywide, such stations shall be operated by GASTEC. In addition, it is scheduled to establish stations providing integrated fueling services (liquid fuel and CNG) countrywide. Also, an integrated plan was made to spread such stations on a large-scale during the next five years in several cities, governorates and the inter-governorates main roads especially in new urban communities and the national road grid.
