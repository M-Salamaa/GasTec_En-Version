Converting a mobile restaurant to run by CNG

The Company prepared and operated a mobile restaurant affiliated to one the working companies in the field of catering facilities to run by CNG by installing two cylinders to operate the restaurant’s stoves and ovens, and installing a pressure-reducing unit. Considering preparing the vehicle with the necessary equipment means of safety and protection in accordance with the requirements of the Egyptian Natural Gas Holding Company through installing a sensor to automatically disconnect the gas in event of gas leakage, also an electric circuit was installed to separate the CNG in the case of vehicle engine operation, as to ensuring that there is no CNG in the restaurant cabin while the vehicle is moving or the mobile restaurant's window is closed.
