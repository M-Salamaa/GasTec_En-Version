CNG Bulk Transportation by Mobile Containers:

GASTEC is considered the first Company in Egypt and Middle East to implement the project of CNG bulk transportation by mobile containers though the Company’s trained labors. GASTEC implemented such project to one of Al-Hurghada hotels that remote from CNG grid by transporting gas daily to feed the hotel from the Company’s station at Al- Hurghada through two gas tankers. The hotel has been equipped with an internal pipeline through which CNG is delivered to generators, boilers and other used devices in the hotel, also units have been installed to reduce gas pressure. In addition, the provision of an additional compressor and fast-filling gas dispenser at Al- Hurghada station in order to fuel tankers transporting CNG to the hotel.

To open external markets to implement CNG bulk transportation projects, GASTEC signed MOU aiming at establishing CNG fueling stations and transporting CNG to feed industrial and commercial activities in the Kingdom of Jordan using Mother-Daughter Station technology. In addition to making the necessary technical and economic studies in cooperation with sister companies in petroleum sector.
