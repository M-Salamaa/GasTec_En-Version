- Available:

The CNG fueling service is available in all CNG fueling stations countrywide 24 hours without stopping as CNG is transferred to stations through the national gas grid.

CNG secures the energy resources for Egypt and helps in achieving a balance on the demand of liquid petroleum products.

 

- Economical:

Saving large amounts of the subsidy provisions allocated by the State to liquid petroleum products.

Attaining economic return for vehicles’ owners upon converting their vehicles to run by CNG through the saving resulting from using CNG instead of liquid fuel.
