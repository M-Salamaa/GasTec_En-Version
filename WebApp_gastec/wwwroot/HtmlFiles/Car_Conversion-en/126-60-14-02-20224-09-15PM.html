Cooperation with Enterprise Development Agency (EDA) to finance the conversion cost

In 2020, the GASTEC affiliated to the Ministry of Petroleum cooperated with EDA (Social Development Fund formerly) to finance vehicles conversion to run by CNG throughout providing soft loans for vehicles’ owners in order to have their vehicles converted to CNG with less interest and longest payment period in which their vehicles are converted at GASTEC stations and branches spreading countrywide by using the latest advanced techniques in such field providing several job vacancies in different governorates.

In Pursuance of enhancing the cooperation with EDA in order to implement Ministry of Petroleum’s strategy of expanding adopting of CNG as an alternative fuel aiming at facilitating on citizens and saving the cost of fuel consumption. During 2020, the Company signed the third and fourth batches of sixth phase to finance the conversion of 5000 vehicles to run by CNG at a value of L.E. 20 million, L.E 10 million for each tranche throughout providing soft loans for vehicles’ owners in order to have their vehicles converted to CNG at less interest and longest payment period in which their vehicles are converted at the Company's stations and branches spreading countrywide by using the latest advanced techniques.

At the beginning of 2021, the Company also signed a new financing phase with EDA, with a value of L.E 100 million on four financing tranches; the value of each batch is L.E.25 million. In addition, the Cooperation with EDA effectively contributes to implementing the plan of generalizing the use of CNG as an alternative fuel, safe and economical.
