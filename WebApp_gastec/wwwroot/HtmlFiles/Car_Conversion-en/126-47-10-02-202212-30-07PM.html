GASTEC developed advanced connections for the use of CNG in fixed engines for industrial purposes, whereas the Company updated number of applications such as converting Forklift to run by CNG. In addition, the Company converted more than 500 forklifts to run by CNG affiliated to Companies such as (Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Toyota, Nissan, Mantrac ... etc.). Also, the Company undertakes the required maintenance works for such Forklifts.
