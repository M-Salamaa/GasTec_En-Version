The Human Resources Department is continuously concerned with improving the employees’ skills and raising their job efficiency through carrying out integrated training plans related to employees’ job path in different fields In line with the company's vision and objectives specially  in light of the difficult circumstances the world is going through -  covid-19 pandemic.

 

30 training programs were carried out for 158 trainees till the end of the first half of 2021

A number of training programs were converted to ELearning in compliance with social distancing rules to avoid infection with the Corona virus.

4 internal training programs were implemented in which 80 trainees from the company’s employees participated. 

Preparing leaders for promotion program to senior administrative positions (assistant general manager - general manager) with the participation of 35 candidates for senior management positions.

Supporting the scientific and practical capabilities of university students through occupying them during summer vacation (6 students).
