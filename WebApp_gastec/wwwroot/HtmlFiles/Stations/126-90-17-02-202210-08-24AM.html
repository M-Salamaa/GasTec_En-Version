For the first time in Egypt and the Middle East, Gastec implemented the first project of fueling mobile station to start providing its services at crowded areas in order to fuel various vehicles by CNG at off grid areas, in which the station being fueling through the nearest gas pipeline or from the nearest CNG main fueling station.

Such station considered nucleus of mobile station for fueling vehicles by CNG, as the procedures of providing 10 mobile stations are underway to spread at different areas to meet customers need, especially at off grid areas for example seasonal consumption areas such as tourist areas, resorts, etc. 

It worth noting that such station characterized by transferring, storing gas amounts reached to 5000 cubic meter, enabling to use in commercial and industrial facilities also in emergency cases and maintenance for gas stations and network. In addition to designing station at small size aiming at increasing number of gas tanks as the space required about 300 m3 .
