In pursuance of the cooperation between the state's ministries and bodies in order to implement the Commands of the political leadership and the government program to convert mass transport vehicles to run by bi-fuel (CNG / gasoline);

Ministry of Petroleum signed a cooperation agreement with the National Service Products Organization (NSPO) to incorporate fueling activity and vehicles conversion to run by bi-fuel system (gasoline / CNG) in the stations of Watania. The concluded agreement stipulates adding fueling activities and vehicles conversion to run by bi-fuel system (gasoline / CNG) in vacant spaces at the Company's stations spreading countrywide through GASTEC and Car Gas.

Contracting with Chill Out Company:

Recently, the Company signed two contracts with Wattania Roads Company (Chill Out) in order to incorporate G-mobility activity in Cairo governorate for two locations, scheduled to be operated during the fourth quarter of 2020 (Al-Musheer axis - October Cityscape).
