Fueling the Public Transport Buses

In the framework of expansion efforts to use CNG as an alternative fuel in vehicles, GASTEC  coordinated with Military 200 Factory, one of of the National Authority for Military Production Companies in order to convert number of (1131) buses affiliated to Public Transport Authority at Cairo and Alexandria to run by CNG instead of diesel on phases during 6 years, including designing, suppling and installing CNG feeding pipes also CNG cylinders and its accessories. In addition to carrying out needed tests for such cylinders at buses conversion location and providing warranty certificates of CNG systems with all accessories. Also, providing all the required spare parts.   

It is worth noting that the Company earlier implemented a project to establish the largest buses fueling station in the Middle East in the garage of the Public Transport Authority in Nasr City, in addition to number of other stations inside such garages in Al-Matareya, Al-Basatin and Port Said Street. Such stations fuel also the customer’s vehicles.
