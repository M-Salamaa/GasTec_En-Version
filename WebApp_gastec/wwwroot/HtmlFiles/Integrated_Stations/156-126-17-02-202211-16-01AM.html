Concerning liquid petroleum products, the Company enhancing cooperation with ENI, considered major partner of GASTEC’s capitalism, also singing an agreement with ENI to establish model stations for liquid fuel branding ENI’s trademark in various cities and governorates as per to the latest executive, administrative and technical specifications applied at ENI’s stations. Such stations operated by GASTEC’s trained labor. Port Said station considered the first ENI’s station to be opened and operated through GASTEC followed by other stations during current year at Al Abbassiya in Cairo, 6th of October, Bein Suef, Tant and other cities.

ENI’s station provides liquid fuel products (gasoline 80 - gasoline 92 - gasoline 95 - diesel) to the public through quad and dual fueling pumps, in addition, other services such as market (eni Market & Cafe), restaurants area, express service and maintenance area, and car wash. Also, advanced services at the station such as car wash water recycling and monitoring the stock of petroleum products of all kinds through ATG system.

The station also provides facilities for charging vehicles with electricity using a charging device through which the vehicle can be charged in a period of 1-3 hours, and the charging value is paid by a magnetic card or an application on smart phones, and after the vehicle is fully charged, a text message is sent to the vehicle’s owner phone.     

  On the other hand, the trial operation of Port Said station coincides with the arrival of ENI’s lubricants products to the Egyptian market for the first time, as GASTEC obtained the right to sell and distribute all ENI’s lubricants products in Egypt.
