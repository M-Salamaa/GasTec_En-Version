The Italian Company Eni considered one of the first pioneers worldwide in the production and manufacture of high-quality lubricants, complying with the requirements of major manufacturers of vehicles, trucks and equipment around the world, which qualified its products to obtain the approval of a large number of major manufacturers in America, Europe and Japan. Therefore, the Company sought to cooperate and coordinate with Eni to offer such distinguished products from different types of lubricants to customers in the Egyptian market to meet the increasing demand for high-quality and high-performance of engine lubricants and industrial lubricants

During 2019, GASTEC delivered the first shipment of ENI industrial lubricants to one of major factories in the industrial zone in 6th of October, in which GASTEC’s lubricants sector officials held an introductory seminar for the operation and maintenance officials of the factory to clarify the advantages of Eni industrial lubricants also agreed to expand the dealing circle in various types of Eni’s  lubricants after the successful trial of the supplied lubricants

On other hand, GASTEC used the first shipment of industrial lubricants in CNG at the Company's station in Dokki, in order to expand the utilization of such lubricants in all types of compressors and various equipment at the Company's stations and centers

This comes within the framework of implementing the strategy of Ministry of Petroleum to provide specialized petroleum products to the requirements of the local market and with the highest quality standards
