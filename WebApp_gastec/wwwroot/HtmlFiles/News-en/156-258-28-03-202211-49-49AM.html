Eni Lubricants and its exclusive agent in Egypt "GASTEC" participated in the first round of the Drifting Competition 2020  REV IT UP Championship, which is held at Family Park in Al-Rehab city.

 

Drifting races are among the most exciting car races that reveal the driver's capabilities and control over the car in tight and sharp turns, while maintaining the time element. The new season 2020 of REV IT UP is the eighth consecutive season of the championship.

 

Approximately 100 competitors participate in the championship each year, divided between the challenges of the REV IT UP Drifting Competition and the REV IT UP Speed ​​Test with specially prepared cars to participate in the championship challenges that take place throughout the year, including: Approximately 10 rounds, which the organizers conclude with a final round at the end of each year.

 

It is worth noting that Eni oils are high-quality, high-performance global oils that are suitable for all engines and equipment, and all industrial purposes, and have obtained the approval of a large number of major manufacturers in America, Europe and Japan. The distribution of Eni lubricants began for the first time in Egypt this year through its exclusive agent "GASTEC Company", which aims to expand the provision of selling and marketing services for oils and liquid fuels in the Egyptian market, in addition to its leadership in the field of converting and supplying vehicles with CNG.
