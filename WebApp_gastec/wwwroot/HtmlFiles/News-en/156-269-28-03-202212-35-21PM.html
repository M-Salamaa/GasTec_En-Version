As part of the Company's continuous efforts to adhere to the application of occupational safety, health and environmental management standards in all operations and management, GASTEC obtained the ISO (45001:2018) certificate in occupational safety and health management, in addition to renewing the ISO (14001:2015) certificate.

 

Eng. /Abdel-Fattah Farhat, GASTEC Chairman and Managing Director, stated that the Company pays great attention to standards of safety, occupational health, environmental protection and quality in all operations, as part of the strategy of the Ministry of Petroleum and Mineral Resources of the necessity of applying the highest international standards for safety and security in all stages of the oil and gas industry.

 

He added that the Company aims to develop work in all departments and keep pace with developments in all fields to provide distinguished services to customers, taking into account international requirements and standards for occupational safety and health, environmental protection and quality.

 

The ISO certificate is one of the most important international certificates for reviewing environmental systems, which is based on ensuring compliance with all international requirements, and meeting the required occupational safety and health standards in operations.

 

The company has previously obtained the ISO 9001 certificate, and GASTEC is the first NGV Company in Egypt to obtain the ISO (45001:2018) certificate in the occupational health and safety management system.
