In pursuance to the cooperation protocol with General Egypt to convert the new G-Long microbus to run by CNG, the cooperation between the two companies resulted in converting 100 microbus as a first stage to operate in bi-fuel cycle (natural gas - liquid fuel).

 

GASTEC conducted advertising campaigns at some of the Company's stations to inform the public about the specifications and advantages of G-Long microbus and the value of savings achieved by the customer through using the CNG-powered microbus and presenting gifts to customers through the two companies.
