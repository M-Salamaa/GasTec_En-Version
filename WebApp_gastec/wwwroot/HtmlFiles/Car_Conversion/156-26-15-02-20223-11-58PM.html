Conversion Plans to alternative fuel (CNG) have recently gained high place a large priority and a growing interest worldwide given the vital and strategic importance of reducing dependence on conventional liquid fuel in energy policies. CNG is considered one of the most important and efficient alternative fuel because of its highly competitive operating costs, its availability in large quantities and its affordable price. CNG is an eco-fuel as well.

After the major success achieved by the Ministry of Petroleum in last gas discoveries such as Zohr field, developing its production facilities in a record time and attaining self-sufficiency of natural gas thus, using CNG in vehicles has great opportunities for expansion and spreading in the upcoming period.
