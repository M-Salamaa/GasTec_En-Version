Vision   

Providing safe scheme for adopting CNG as an alternative fuel & an integrated fueling service.

 

GASTEC's vision copes with its role in contributing to the implementation of the petroleum sector's vision to turn Egypt into a regional hub for trading oil and gas.

 

Mission 

The Company aims at gaining the customers’ loyalty and trust, achieving competitive advantage and added value for attaining sustainable development for community and shareholders through qualifying and training distinctive staff contributing to achieving the Ministry of Petroleum’s strategy for Egypt 2030 vision.

 

Goals

• Spreading the use of CNG as an alternative fuel through vehicles conversion to run by CNG implementing the Ministry of Petroleum’s strategy.

 

• Providing integrated fueling services by CNG and liquid fuel and other services meeting the customers’ needs countrywide.

 

• Providing Premium Commercial and Industrial Lubricants to cover the local market requirements.

 

• Contributing to implementing the Ministry of Petroleum plans of Transporting CNG to the off-grid areas.

 

Values

2. Safety and Occupational Health.

3. Work Ethics.

4. Employees Empowerment.

5. Working with team spirit.

6. Efficiency and Excellence.

7. Innovation.

8. Transparency and Trust.
