Auditing Stations' Performance and Equipment Status using Smartphones

Performance & Condition Monitoring Program has been implemented, it is an "application" installs on computers and smartphones to maintenance engineers and station’s labors to follow up and monitor station’s performance on all levels of sales, conversion, breakdowns, maintenance, electricity  consumption and lubricants, aiming at assessing the efficiency of the Company's equipment and stations, achieving sales plan and measuring  various performance indicators for individuals and equipment through constant connection between smartphone and station.
