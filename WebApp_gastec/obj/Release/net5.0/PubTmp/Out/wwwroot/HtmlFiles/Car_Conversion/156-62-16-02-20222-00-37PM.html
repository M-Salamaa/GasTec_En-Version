CNG operation system is very safe, as the gas cylinder installed in the vehicle is equipped with an automatic shutdown system closing the cylinder to prevent gas leakage, also the cylinder is made of an alloy of special steel to endure the high pressure of CNG inside it. The most important safety factors in the conversion kit group represents as follows:

 

 

1 -Gas Pipe & Fitting:

•    It is made of steel without welds, raising its durability of high pressures and avoiding gas leaking.

 

•    The gas pipe is covered with a plastic layer to prevent the accumulation of static electricity charges.

 

 

2 -Gas Valve:

•    It prevents any gas flow in case of stopping the engine or the vehicle breakdown.

 

3 - Change over Switch & ECU:

 

•    Through which the vehicle's operation is converted from CNG to gasoline automatically in case of CNG permeation, preventing the sudden stopping of the engine, which may cause vehicle malfunction while driving (synchronous injection system).

 

4 -Filling Valve:

 

•  Contains a non-return valve to secure the vehicle’s fueling process by CNG at the stations and prevent leaking when completing the fueling process.

 

5 - Ventilation Hoses:

 

•    The hoses ensure removing any gas leaking from the gas connections of the cylinder to outside the vehicle and preventing the accumulation of CNG in the vehicle’s trunk.

 

6 - Cylinder Valve:

•    It allows releasing the excess pressure, whether due to the high temperature surrounding the cylinder body or the high pressure of gas inside the cylinder.

 

•    When gas flows out of the cylinder in large quantities as a result of a break in the gas pipe, it closes automatically.

 

7 - Gas Cylinder:

•    It is designed according to International specifications and Iso Standard 11349.

 

•    The cylinder is manufactured in accordance with high safety standards and under the supervision of international inspection bodies as well, it has undergone numerous tests during the production phases to ensure its safety in vehicles and its ability to endure the high temperatures and shocks that the vehicle may be exposed to while driving.

 

•    Gas cylinders are subject to periodic examination tests to restore their validity every three years throughout GASTEC’s accredited testing center, where they are exposed to high pressure equivalent to one and a half times the operating pressure also, examination with sound waves to ensure their safety during their service in the vehicle.
