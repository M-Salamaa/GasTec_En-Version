State’s benefits from using CNG in vehicles:

- Contributing to implementing energy rationalization policies and reducing subsidy provisions allocated to liquid petroleum products, to which the State was allocating huge sums in the budget, and starts rationalizing it gradually during the previous few years.

 

- Contributing effectively to implementing environmental conservation and air quality policies in the Egyptian cities, whereas CNG reducing the harmful emissions resulting from vehicles, leading to maintaining citizens’ health.

- Reducing dependence on importing liquid fuel products (gasoline and diesel) required for the local market, whereas Egypt produces about half of its needs and imports the rest. In addition, the expansion in using CNG would contribute significantly to reducing the import of such products, and thus saving hard currency.

 

-  Optimizing the economic benefit from Egypt's natural resources and maximizing the added value of such resources.

 

-Contributing to implementing projects of developing the transport sectors in cooperation with other relevant ministries in the state.

Given that CNG is an eco-fuel and highly efficient. Therefore, its use has been expanded worldwide during the previous years in new sectors such as; river and maritime transport, railway tractors, and various ground services units in airports.
