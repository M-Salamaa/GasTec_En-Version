The price of conversion ranges from L.E. 5000 for carburetors and about L.E. 7500 for electronic injection vehicles also the cost varies according to the conversion kit type and the number of cylinders that would be installed in vehicles. The actual cost of converting vehicle is much more than that and is borne by Ministry of Petroleum aiming at a continuous encouragement for citizens to convert their vehicles to CNG.

 

Facilities are being provided for customers to reimbursement conversion value through multiple systems of installments with simplified contracting procedures such as copy of the ID card, vehicle license and a recent receipt of "Electricity - Gas - Water". In addition, immediate gifts are given to customers when paying cash.

The value of direct saving for citizen who convert his vehicle to run by CNG

CNG is an economical fuel. Currently the price of CNG is L.E. 3.75 per cubic meter, while the price of gasoline (80) is L.E. 7.00 and gasoline (92) is L.E.8.25

 

In case a citizen uses CNG in his vehicle, he could achieve savings of about L.E. 975 per month at an average daily consumption of 10 liters of gasoline "80", while the saving rate reaches of about L.E. 1350 per month in case of using gasoline "92", thus the customer can refund the conversion value from the savings of using CNG during 3 to 6 months at the average consumption rate, considering that the more the average daily consumption of gasoline, the less the payback period.
