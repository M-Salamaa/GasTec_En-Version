Using CNG as an alternative, clean and eco fuel in Egypt began as a pilot project in one of the companies affiliated to petroleum sector in 1985. After the success of such project, Ministry of Petroleum founded in the mid-nineties of the last century two affiliated companies to establish fueling stations and vehicles conversion centers to run by CNG. The project achieved a great success whereas Egypt is ranked within the pioneering countries worldwide in NGV field.

Given the continuous support provided by Ministry of Petroleum and Egyptian Natural Gas Holding Company to NGV companies, the number of vehicles running with CNG increased, and the grid of CNG fueling stations in Egypt expanded countrywide. In addition, the various uses of CNG, including its operation in sectors and several commercial and industrial institutions, have developed.

GASTEC is the pioneering company in the field of using CNG as an alternative fuel in vehicles in Egypt and the Middle East as the Company has been working in NGV field for more than 20 years, and owns the largest grid of fueling stations in Egypt including a large base of compressors with different capacities. Moreover, number of stations under construction, and vehicles conversion centers to run by CNG with different capacities. In addition, specialized centers in testing gas cylinders. It is worth noting that the Company is ranked the largest market share among NGV companies in Egypt.
