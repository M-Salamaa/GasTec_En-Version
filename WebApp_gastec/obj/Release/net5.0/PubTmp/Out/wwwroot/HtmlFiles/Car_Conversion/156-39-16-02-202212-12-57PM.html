1- The technical inspection of vehicle at conversion center by specialized technicians to make sure of the engine’s validity to run by CNG, whereas its efficiency must exceed 70%, and to determine the appropriate conversion kit and the cylinder capacity to be installed. In addition, notes are taken in the examination report, if any.

2- Receiving the vehicle from the customer on the day of conversion, and making sure to avoid all the notes in the examination report before the conversion process.

3- Installing conversion kit, gas cylinder, and all necessary connections.

The compliance with the standard specifications is a must when installing conversion kit components for protection, commitment to occupational safety and maintaining high safety rates in maintenance and operation processes.

4-The vehicle is fueled with CNG then it is checked for leak so as to ensure that all connections are tightly secured.

5- The vehicle is controlled with special control devices to ensure that the exhaust proportions are within the permissible limits according to specifications.

6-The customer has the freedom to shift between CNG and gasoline system in the converted vehicle, whereas vehicle conversion process is carried out by adding the parts of CNG without any modification in the basic fuel cycle (gasoline), allowing the customer to run the vehicle with bi-fuel system (CNG / gasoline). Shifting between the two systems through a conversion switch installed in the vehicle's panel. There is no change or modification in any part of the engine during the conversion process.

7- Handing over the vehicle to the customer after trying the CNG system in addition to giving him all documents; the warranty certificate, certificate of origin for traffic and a maintenance manual.
