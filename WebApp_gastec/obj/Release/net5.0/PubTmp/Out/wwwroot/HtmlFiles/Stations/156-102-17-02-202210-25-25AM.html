Safety and Occupational Health

Gastec has obtained the ISO 45001:2018 certificate in the Department of Safety and Occupational Health, which is one of the most important international certificates for reviewing environmental systems, concerned with ensuring that all international requirements regarding the required safety and occupational health standards are met in operations.

Gastec is the first Egyptian CNG company to obtain the ISO certificate (45001:2018) in the occupational health and safety management system.
