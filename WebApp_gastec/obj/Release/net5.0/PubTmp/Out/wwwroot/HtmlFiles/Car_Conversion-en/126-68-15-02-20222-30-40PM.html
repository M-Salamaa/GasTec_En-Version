In pursuance to GASTEC’s effort to support and encourage boosting the local manufacturing of equipment and materials used in CNG activities as an alternative fuel in Egypt in the light of the National Project of Egyptian State to spread adopting CNG in G-mobility.

Since years, the Company implemented a gradual policy for local manufacturing of equipment and materials used in NGV activities aiming at providing the imported materials from abroad locally, saving hard currency, and coping with the State policy towards decreasing the trade deficit and Ministry of Petroleum strategy in order to maximize local manufacturing inside the sector also fulfilling the operation needs of different spare parts.

On the other hand, the Company targeted a gradual localization of technology and know-how in such field, in addition to preparing trained and qualified technical cadres.

In this concern, the Company manufactured and developed some vehicles' systems and conversion equipment as (Timing Advance processor - Injector emulator - Carburetor CNG switch - Injection CNG switch).
