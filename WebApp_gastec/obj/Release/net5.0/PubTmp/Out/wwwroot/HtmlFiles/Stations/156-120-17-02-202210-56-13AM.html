Study of transporting CNG to a fertilizers factory in Aswan Governorate

Coordination was made with the Natural Gas Holding Company to feed a fertilizer factory in Aswan with gas quantities estimated at 12,000 m3 per day.

Coordination is underway to complete contracting with the factory, in cooperation with the concessionaire company, where the price of  internal networks has been submitted, and the Holding Company’s inspection is underway to complete the contract, as the factory will be fed from  GASTEC station in Qena (national recruitment), which contains excess capacity sufficient to feed the factory.
