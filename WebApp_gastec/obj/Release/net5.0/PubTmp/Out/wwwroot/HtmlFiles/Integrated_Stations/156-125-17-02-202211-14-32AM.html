Port Said operation capacity is 900 m3/h with fueling capacity 1000 vehicles per day. Such station operated by solar energy cells distributed on the roof of administrative building and charging canopy with annual total amount 150.5 MW/h and annual total saving about L.E. 218 thousands. 

GASTEC’s station at Port Said considered the first integrated fueling station run by CNG ,liquid fuel and electric charging in Egypt and Middle East through which the Company introduces modern and updated design for its new stations, such design distinguished with installing gas facilities in terms of compressors, gas tanks and its accessories on the top of the fueling canopy, availing vacant spaces at stations can be used in various activities for customer services, contributing in developing the infrastructure of CNG fueling facilities and supporting the spread of CNG fueling stations. In addition to updating the current shape of gas dispensers at fueling stations aiming at providing space at fueling region and increasing safety rates in which all dispenser components installed at the top of the canopy.
