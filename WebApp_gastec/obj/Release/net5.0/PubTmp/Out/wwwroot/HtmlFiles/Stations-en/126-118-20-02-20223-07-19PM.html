Feeding a textile factory with CNG

The company has completed supplying and feeding a textile factorie temporarly  for a  4 months period until the completion of the  external network connections by the concessionaire company with CNG in daily quantities of 4000 m3 from  GASTEC station located in tenth of Ramadan by special mobile containers to transport Compressed Natural Gas to feed industrial facilities and commercial areas far from the gas line, through the company's trained labor.
